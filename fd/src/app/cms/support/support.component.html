At PassedOn.com we believe that Every life has a story, and, that those stories need to be both told and preserved.

PassedOn.com is Australia and the world’s first integrated social media website offering tributes to those who have died by providing a platform for the remembrance of loved ones.

PassedOn.com is the online source for current local and national obituary news and a supportive community where friends and family can come together during times of loss and grieving to honour the memories of their loved ones with lasting personal tributes.

PassedOn provides a mechanism for the remembrance of those that have passed, by those that remain for all future generations to enjoy.

“Every life has a story”

Nick Milillo and Peter Gibson are founders and directors of PassedOn. Our mission is to ensure your memories are treated with the upmost respect by representing them in a manner that honours the deceased. By providing a platform which is pleasing on the eye, easy to use, a place you want to return too, we hope that we have achieved this on your behalf. We hope we have achieved this by also ensuring your Tribute is not morbid and Advertisement free.
