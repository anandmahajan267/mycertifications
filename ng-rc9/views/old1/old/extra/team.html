Team - Meet our team

Trex Lim
Head of System Architect

Trex is the Head of System Architect at RC9. He is responsible for the evolvement of the design and development of RC9's systems. He is a defining figure in the company who defines the system requirements and creates high level architectural specifications. He ensures scalability, functionality and integration of RC9's vision with existing systems and platforms.

Trex began his humble journey as a website developer at the age of 15. With his evergrowing passion for technology, it triggered him to explore beyond the scope of website developer. Over the years, he acquired various technology skills ranging from mobile app to server development also led a team in the annual Microsoft Imagine Cup 2010 and came in Top 6th worldwide. Prior to RC9, he was also a recipient of the ACE-startup grant for his mobile gaming app venture. In addition, he is also a certified holder of SAP and Oracle Cloud ERP.

Krystie Ma
Chief Marketing Officer (CMO)

Krystie is the CMO at RC9. She is a highly motivated individual that focuses on instrumental marketing and innovative customer acquisition strategies for the company. Accompanied with a strong passion for the fruition of a successful vehicle sharing economy, it propels her beyond her role and exudes dynamic positivity throughout the team.

Coupled with vast experiences in marketing and partnerships previously for some of the biggest malls and well known brands such as HTC, Samsung, SPH in South East Asia, she is capable of sourcing for new opportunities and effective channels of distribution needed to increase the exposure of RC9 abroad. She possesses leadership qualities and analytical skills needed for planning for business units.

Lim Guo Hong
Co-Founder

Guo Hong is the co-founder at RC9. He is responsible for the IT strategy to attain RC9's vision of making personal transportation more affordable and safer with transparency in mind. He strongly believes in using the capabilities of technology to empower and inspire commuters from the way they move around.

He has more than 9 years of experience in Information Technology Field gained from renowned multinational technological companies. During his tenure with Microsoft Corporation, he tailor-made training and marketing campaigns for APAC Microsoft Subsidiaries and oversaw future world changing prototyping projects for Microsoft Regional School Technology Innovation Centre. He is currently pursing Bachelor (Honors) of Computing in Information System from National University of Singapore. In his free time, he would code mobile applications and blog on technological articles.

Daryl Png
Co-Founder

Daryl is the co-founder at RC9. He drives the company's vision, strategy and growth along with a strong desire to make personal transportation more transparent.
